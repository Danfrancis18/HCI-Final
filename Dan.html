Spaghetti Carbonara

Description: A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. Creamy, savory, and quick to prepare.
Ingredients: Spaghetti, eggs, pancetta, Parmesan cheese, black pepper.
Instructions: Cook pasta. Fry pancetta. Mix eggs and cheese. Combine all with pasta and pancetta off heat.
